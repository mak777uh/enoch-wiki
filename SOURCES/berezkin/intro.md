30

6.
Origin and interpretation of culture elements related to agriculture and to
inadequate forms of subsistence and economic activity before the establishment
of the present norms.

60
(or 6a). Origin of different culture elements not related to subsistence.

as protagonists of cosmological stories, metamorphoses.

particular qualities (physical or professional).
