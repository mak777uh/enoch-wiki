# Viracocha

## What this source gives us

This source is a useful comparative entry for the Andean creator and culture hero Viracocha. It highlights creation, civilizing travel, destruction of the disobedient, and the sacred geography of the Andes.

## Why it matters

- It gives us a creator / culture hero motif outside the Old World.
- It is linked to sacred geography, especially Lake Titicaca.
- It includes teaching, ordering, and punitive destruction.
- It is useful for comparison with divine civilizers, wandering sages, and building / order motifs.

## Key motifs

- creator and ruler
- emergence from water
- civilizing journey
- punishment of the disobedient
- sacred geography
