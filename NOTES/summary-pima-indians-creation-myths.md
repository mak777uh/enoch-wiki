# The Pima Indians: abstracts of myths: summary

## What the text is

This is a short comparative myth collection focused on Pima material. It preserves creation-related mythic memory and gives us a North American point of comparison for origin and world-making.

## What it does

The source presents myth not as long epic, but as compact remembered abstract. That makes it useful as a different kind of source: it shows how myth can survive in summary form, not only in grand literary epics.

## Why it matters for the base

The source is useful because it broadens the comparative field and shows that even short myth abstracts can carry:

- creation logic
- sacred memory
- origin structure
- local world-making
